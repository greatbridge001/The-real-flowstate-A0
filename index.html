Money Rules for Abednego

Pay Yourself First

The moment income arrives, move savings to a separate account before spending anything. What you don't see, you don't spend.

The 72-Hour Rule

Any non-essential purchase above KES 2,000 — wait 72 hours. Most impulse desires disappear on their own.

Build Emergency Fund First

Target 3–6 months of expenses (KES 50k–150k) in M-Shwari or a money market fund before putting money into assets.

Invest in Assets That Pay You

Skills, digital products, websites, and courses — every shilling you put into an income-generating asset works while you sleep.
