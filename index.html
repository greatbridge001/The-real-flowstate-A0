About Greatbridge Academy

Three decades of nurturing excellence in education

Our Heritage

Our History

Greatbridge Academy was founded in 1993 by a group of educators who believed that quality education should be accessible to all children regardless of their background. What began as a small community school with a handful of students has grown into one of the most respected secondary schools in the Rift Valley region.

Through decades of dedicated service, consistent investment in infrastructure and human capital, and an unwavering commitment to academic standards, Greatbridge Academy has produced alumni who serve in all sectors of Kenya's national life — in medicine, law, business, public service, and the arts.

Today, with over 1,200 students and more than 85 teaching and support staff, we continue to build on this proud legacy while embracing the innovations required to prepare students for an ever-changing world.

Our Approach

Curriculum

We follow the Kenya National Curriculum aligned with the Kenya Institute of Curriculum Development (KICD) framework, offering both the CBC and KCSE pathways.

Junior School (CBC)

Our junior school implements the Competency Based Curriculum (CBC) with emphasis on inquiry-based learning, creativity, and practical skills development.

  • Grade 7, 8, and 9
  • Seven learning areas
  • Project-based and portfolio assessment
  • Co-curricular integration

Senior School (KCSE)

Form 1 to Form 4 students follow the KCSE curriculum with a comprehensive range of subjects across core and elective clusters.

  • Forms 1 through 4
  • Languages, Sciences, Humanities, Technical
  • KCSE examinations through KNEC
  • University guidance and placement support

Life at Greatbridge

More Than Just Academics

At Greatbridge Academy, we believe that education extends well beyond the classroom. Our rich co-curricular programme gives students the opportunity to discover their passions, develop leadership skills, and build lifelong friendships.

From championship sports teams to award-winning debate clubs, from science olympiad teams to cultural dance troupes — there is something for every student at Greatbridge.

All students are encouraged to participate in at least one club and one sporting activity each term.

Admissions

Begin your journey to excellence at Greatbridge Academy

How to Apply

Admission Process

We welcome applications from academically motivated students who wish to join our community of learners.

1

Download Application Form

Download and complete the admission application form from our downloads page or collect a physical copy from the school office.

2

Submit Documents

Attach certified copies of birth certificate, previous school reports (last 3 terms), and KCPE results where applicable.

3

Entrance Assessment

Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an entrance assessment covering Mathematics and English.

4

Interview and Offer

Successful candidates and their parents will be invited for an interview. Admission letters are issued within two weeks.

5

Fee Payment and Reporting

Pay the prescribed fees and report on the indicated date with all required items from the school kit list.
